<h1> VOCA </h1>

<p> **Voca** is an accessibility-focused web app that helps users — especially people with ADHD — capture ideas effortlessly. Instead of typing, users simply speak, and Voca instantly turns their thoughts into organized text and AI-generated cover art.
Voca removes friction, reduces overwhelm, and helps ideas become real. </p>
